        Search Criteria Help

        With our legal research engine, accessing past judgments and preparing a case defence has never been easier.
        Citation Input of precise citation; in citation-based searches enhances the search optimisation's. e.g. 'P L D 1967 Supreme Court 486', '1996 SCMR 883', 'PLD 1990 Supreme Court (AJ&K) 1' & '1999 M L D 657'
        Judges Take advantage of the autofill options when searching for specific or multiple judges to save time, and research with ease.
        Courts Increase the potency of your search results by selecting relevant courts from the filters.
        Statutes When searching for judgements with particular statutes, inputting the resonant statute aids in providing best results. e.g. 'Pakistan Penal Code, 1860', 'Delimitation of Constituencies Rules, 2015'' & 'The Representation Of The People Act, 1976 [Section 57]'
        Practice Area Refine your search results by selecting specific or multiple Practice Areas through the available filters.
        Year Optimise your search results by selecting singular or multiple Years through the filters.
        Natural Language Search Lastly, feel at ease to use the 'free-text-search' by entering any keywords or phrases to find case relevant judgements, it is free speech not just legal jargon.
